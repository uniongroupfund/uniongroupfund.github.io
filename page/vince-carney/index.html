Vince Carney · Partner

Union Group Fund · Kansas City, MO

Vince Carney is the founder of Union Group Fund, a company that is driven to change how small, bootstrapped software companies are funded. He has worked as a software engineer and online business entrepreneur since 2006, focusing on creating and investing in online platforms with effortless customer-centric design.

Vince graduated with honors from Rockhurst University, with a B.S. in Business Administration. He is married to Shelley and has four children - Johanna, Carolyn, Ben & Luke - and resides in Kansas City, Missouri. When he is not working hard to develop and invest in online businesses that customers love, you will find him spending time with his family, or on the golf course.
